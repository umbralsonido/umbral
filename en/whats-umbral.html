WHAT'S UMBRAL?

We are sound professionals.

We recognise Sound as a powerful and useful tool to communicate with vast creative, expressive and imaginative possibilities.

As a collective, we work to develop the strategies that best adapt to each stage of a narrative project from a Sound point of view.

The interrelationship between these stages is fundamental, and we want to be as involved as possible in each one of them.
