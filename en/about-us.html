about us

ALDONZA CONTRERAS

Degree in Audiovisual Arts and, an MA in Visual Anthropology.

I have worked in Sound for cinema both in production and in post-production, as a sound designer, editor, foley artist and sound engineer. I have experience in feature and short films in fictions, animation and documentaries. I have also explored the field of Sound for theatre. I am very interested in collaborating with other artists and exploring sound possibilities in different formats, from cinema to sound installations and podcast.

I am very fascinated by the convergence between art and science. I have found in Sound a very potent medium of expression.

RODRIGO FLORES ESQUINCA

Born in Mexico City, I've worked with Sound in several content formats since 2007.

In human voice, I find an emotional vehicle, which speaks to us from within the deepest part of a human being.

Recently, I'm interested in its practice through documentaries and podcasts.

CESAR ZAYAS

Born in Hermosillo, Son.

I graduated from the Image and Sound Department (UDG) in 2010. In film production, I have specialised myself in Production Sound, both in fiction and documentary. To me, Sound, when used in its full expression, is a powerful tool to convey and perceive external stimuli uniquely.

I am currently exploring sound post-production and ADR work.
